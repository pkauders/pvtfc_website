Summer Camps

Weekly tennis camps for ages 4–18, June through August

Overview

A Summer on the Courts

Our summer tennis camps are the perfect way for kids to stay active, make friends, and develop their tennis skills in a fun, supportive environment. Camps run weekly from mid-June through early August.

Little Aces Camp

Ages 4–6
$175/week
Half Day 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM

A gentle introduction to tennis through games, mini-court play, and coordination activities. Perfect for first-timers who want to have fun and burn energy.

What's Included

  • Foam ball tennis games
  • Coordination & agility activities
  • Snack break
  • Friday mini-tournament & prizes

Ages 7–10
$195/week
Half Day 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Build real tennis skills in a high-energy camp focused on stroke development, footwork, and friendly competition.

What's Included

  • Stroke technique clinics

  • Match play & games
  • Snack break
  • Friday tournament & awards

Ages 10–14
