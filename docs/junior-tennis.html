Junior Programs

Junior Tennis

Six levels from first-timers to tournament players

Our Levels

Find Your Level

Each level is designed to develop skills progressively. Sessions run 6–8 weeks. Contact us for current session dates and availability.

Red Ball

Ages 4–7

Introduction to tennis fundamentals, coordination, and essential elements of the game. Uses small racquets and foam balls on a smaller court to build confidence and love for the sport.

Tue/Thu 5:00–6:00 PM, Sat 10:00–11:00 AM

What You'll Learn

  • Hand-eye coordination
  • Basic forehand & backhand
  • Movement & balance
  • Fun games & teamwork

Green Ball

Ages 7–10

Focuses on stroke technique and footwork using small to medium racquets with low-pressure balls. Players begin rallying and learning court positioning.

Wed 4:30–5:30 PM, Fri 4:00–5:00 PM, Sat 11:00 AM–12:00 PM

What You'll Learn

  • Stroke technique development
  • Footwork fundamentals
  • Rally consistency
  • Introduction to scoring

Yellow Ball

Ages 10+

Emphasis on tennis fundamentals, stroke technique and development, coordination, and competitive drilling. Players transition to full-court play with regulation balls.

Mon 5:00–6:30 PM, Thu 6:00–7:30 PM, Sat 11:00 AM–12:30 PM

What You'll Learn

  • Full-court play
  • Serve development
  • Competitive drilling
  • Match play introduction

Bronze

Ages 14+

Ideal for beginner high school players building foundational skills. Develops proper technique, consistency, and competitive awareness for school team preparation.

Tue/Wed 7:00–8:30 PM, Sat 1:00–2:30 PM

What You'll Learn

  • High school team preparation
  • Foundational technique
  • Singles & doubles strategy
  • Competitive readiness

Silver

Ages 14+ (Pro Staff Approval)

Balance of high-quality instruction, competitive play, and stroke development with emphasis on skill advancement. Requires pro staff approval for placement.

Tue/Wed 7:00–8:30 PM, Sat 1:00–2:30 PM

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced stroke development
  • Tactical match play
  • Tournament preparation
  • Mental toughness

Junior Elite Performance

By Approval Only

Advanced program featuring high-impact drills and match play strategy for tournament-level and varsity players. Invitation-only based on pro staff evaluation.

Wed 6:30–8:30 PM

What You'll Learn

  • Tournament-level training
  • Advanced match strategy
  • High-intensity drilling
  • Performance analytics
